Political apathy among students is a big problem.

We're here to fix it.

The Students Against Political Apathy Movement is about fixing the political apathy that has infected students around the country through engaging, nonpartisan educational programs for students to learn the basics of politics and develop political opinions.

MEET THE DREAM TEAM

The SAPA Team is responsible for maintaining the movement. From social media, to live events, to the website you're on now, the SAPA Team makes it happen. The SAPA Team is 100% composed of bright students from around the country, dedicated to fighting political apathy among students.

Our Divisions


The SAPA Team is composed of several divisions that make our movement possible.

Operations

 

Operations is responsible for managing overall operation of the project as well as timelines and goals.

Public Relations

 

Public Relations manages outreach and connections, so our movement can reach even further.

Program Development

 

Program Development is behind the creation of new, nonpartisan, engaging programs that educate students on politics and government.

Digital Media

 

Digital Media works behind the scences to create powerful animations and graphics and code complex scripts.
